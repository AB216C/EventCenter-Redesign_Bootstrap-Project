Burna Boy's birth name is Damini Ebunoluwa Ogulu. Born July 2, 1991 in Nigera, Africa. He is a singer, song writer, performer and record producer. He was considered the first african artist to have two albums earn over 1 billion streams on Spotify. In 2023, Burna Boy received his fourth award as the Best International Act at the 2023 BET Awards
